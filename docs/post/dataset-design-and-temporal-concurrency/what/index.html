Metrics & Dimensions

First, let’s define the key measures and related metrics listed in the problem statement:

  • \(\gamma_1\): Average length of stay (show)

    \(\gamma(x)=\bar{x};\enspace x := \big(t_{i+1}-t_{i}\big)\)

  • \(\gamma_2\): Monthly counts of lapses in medication adherence

\(f(x, m)=\sum_{i=1}^{m}{x_m};\enspace x:=\Big\{\matrix{0, \text{No lapse}\\1, \text{Lapse}}; \enspace m:= \text{month index}\)

  • \(\gamma_3\): Cumulative count of lapses in medication adherence

\(f(\gamma_2, m)=\sum_{i=1}^{m}{\gamma_2|i\le{m}}\)

  • \(\gamma_4\): Number of unique members

\(f(x)=\#x;\enspace x:=\text{the set of unduplicated member identifiers}\)

  • \(\gamma_5\): Total expenditures

\(f(x)=\sum{x};\enspace x:=\text{cost}\)
